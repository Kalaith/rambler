# 🧩 The Rambler - Feature Roadmap

## ✍️ Capture (Core)
- **Raw Text**: Smooth, distraction-free writing.
- **Voice-to-Text**: One-click basic quality capture.

## 🪞 Extraction (Core)
- **Useful Kernels**: AI extracts key insights and informative results from raw, nonsensical text.
- **Summarization**: Neutral bullet-point summaries.
- **Topic Detection**: Automated tagging of mentioned themes.
- **Thought Reflection**: Surface questions and flagged ideas.

## 🧠 Intelligence (Clarity Tier)
- **Cross-Ramble Intelligence**: Detect recurring themes over time.
- **Deeper Extraction Modes**: 
    - "Turn this into project notes"
    - "Extract story seeds"
    - "Find assumptions I'm making"
- **Memory Lens**: Timeline view of ideas evolving.

## 🧰 Expansion Modules (Opt-In)
- **ADHD Mode**: Shorter summaries, "what matters right now" focus.
- **Writer Mode**: Plot hooks, character fragments, worldbuilding.
- **Developer Mode**: Feature ideas, risk lists, architecture notes.
