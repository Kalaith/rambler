# 💰 The Rambler - Monetization & Privacy

## 🟦 Paid Tier: “Clarity” (~$3–5/month)
You’re not paying to think. You’re paying to organize thinking over time.
- **Cross-Ramble Intelligence**: Theme detection and thought evolution tracking.
- **Deeper Extractions**: Project notes, story seeds, and assumption finding.
- **Lifetime Option**: A one-time purchase ($20–40) for those who hate subscriptions.

## 🪙 Credits & Allowance
- Monthly free allowance for AI processing.
- Simple, non-expiring top-ups for heavy use.
- Full transparency on the cost of each operation.

## 🔒 Privacy & Transparency
- **Server Storage**: Data is stored in plain text on the server. Security should not be assumed during this phase.
- **Data Ownership**: Manual delete means actually deleted. Local export (Markdown/JSON) available.
- **Future-Proof**: Planned "local-only mode" for maximum privacy.
